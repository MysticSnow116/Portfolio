About Me

Hello my name is Alexander Hernandez, I'm an aspiring graphic designer living around Raliegh who enjoys making drawings, illustrations, logos and package designs. Currently, I'm pursuing my associate degree in advertising & graphic design at Wake Technical Community College. My favorite type of design work would be creating vector artwork that involves the use of many different shapes and colors to create a stunning design using simple shapes. Since a young age, I have always been a creative type of person always drawing and imagining many different ideas. Now that I'm becoming a graphic designer, I can show and create the many ideas I have going through my mind.
